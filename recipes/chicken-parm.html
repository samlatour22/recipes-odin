Chicken Parm

chicken parm

Fried breaded chicken breast topped with marinara and parmesan cheese.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Bread Crums
  • Tomatos
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Eggs
  • Flour
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Crack eggs into bowl, add flour to plate and add bread crumbs to bowl.
  2. Pat chicken breast in flour, dunk chicken in eggs and coat chicken breast with bread.
  3. Make marinara with tomatos.
  4. Fry chicken at med-high heat until golden brown.
  5. Top with marinara, shredded parmesan cheese.
  6. At low heat add lid to pan to melt the cheese.
  7. ENJOY!!!
