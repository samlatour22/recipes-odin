French Toast

Fried cinnamon and egg soaked bread, topped with powdered sugar, syrup and strawberries.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Bread Crums
  • Powdered Sugar
  • Powdered Cinnamon
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Maple Syrup
  • Strawberries

Steps

  1. Crack eggs into bowl, add 1 tablespoon of milk.
  2. Dunk bread in eggs and milk combo.
  3. Sprinkle cinnamon on top of bread.
  4. Butter pan and fry at med-high heat on both sides until golden brown.
  5. Sprinkle powdered sugar on top of toast.
  6. Top with maple syrup and strawberries.
  7. ENJOY!!!
