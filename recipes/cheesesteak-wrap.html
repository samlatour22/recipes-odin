Cheesesteak Wrap

Minced steak, onions, green peppers and cheese in a tortilla wrap

Ingredients

  • Steak
  • Onions
  • Green Peppers
  • Cheese
  • Tortilla

Steps

  1. Fry chopped onions and green peppers at medium-high heat.
  2. Add steak at medium-high heat until cooked
  3. Sprinkle cinnamon on top of bread.
  4. Fry tortilla until darkened in another pan.
  5. Add cheese to steak and add a lid to melt cheese.
  6. Fill tortilla with steak, onion, green pepper and cheese filling.
  7. ENJOY!!!
